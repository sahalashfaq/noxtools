Privacy Policy

At NoxTools https://noxtools.com,safeguarding your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to ensuring that your personal information is respected and secure when you interact with our website or app. For general visitors browsing our site, we do not collect any personal data. However, we do gather information from users who actively engage with the site, such as when you submit comments, feedback, or participate in other site-related interactions.
This collected information is solely used to improve the quality and functionality of our platform. It allows us to tailor our services based on user preferences and provide you with an enhanced experience that aligns with your interests.

It is important to note that we strictly offer articles and general information to our users. We never ask for sensitive data, such as credit card numbers or personal financial information, ensuring that your experience on our platform remains both informative and secure.

Third-Party Disclosure and Privacy Protection Acts:

At NoxTools, we take your privacy seriously and adhere to strict policies regarding the sharing of Personally Identifiable Information (PII). We do not sell, trade, or transfer your PII to any outside parties.
California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA):
CalOPPA, the first state law in the U.S. requiring websites to display a privacy policy, ensures transparency regarding data collection. This law affects not just California-based businesses, but any company operating websites that collect PII from California consumers. In accordance with CalOPPA, we commit to the following practices:
Users may visit our website anonymously.
Once our privacy policy is created, a link to it will be provided on our home page or the first significant page users encounter.
Our Privacy Policy link will include the word "Privacy" and will be easy to find.
Additionally, you will be notified of any changes to our Privacy Policy on the designated page. You can also modify your personal information by logging into your account.
Do Not Track (DNT) Signals and Behavioral Tracking:
We respect DNT signals and ensure that no cookies are planted or advertisements used when a DNT mechanism is enabled in a user’s browser. Furthermore, we do not permit third-party behavioral tracking on our site.
Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):
The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) mandates that parents have control over the collection of personal information from children under 13 years old. NoxTools complies with COPPA regulations, and we do not specifically market our services to children under 13.
